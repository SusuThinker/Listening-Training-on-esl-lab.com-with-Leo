## Story 1 ｜[Homestay in the USA](https://www.esl-lab.com/easy/homestay/)  词汇汇总

#### Part 1 核心单词（含完整词族拓展）

##### 1\. Core Nouns 核心名词

1. **dorms /dɔːmz/ n\.（复数）**
**English Definition**：informal short form of dormitory; residential buildings for students at college or university
**中文释义**：宿舍（口语）
完整词族：dormitory /ˈdɔːmɪtri/ n\.（正式）学生宿舍
例句：Are you going to live in the dorms at college next semester?
译文：你下学期打算住学校宿舍吗？

2. **tourism /ˈtʊərɪzəm/ n\.**
**English Definition**：the commercial organization and operation of holidays and visits to places of interest for travelers
**中文释义**：旅游业；观光活动
完整词族：tour /tʊə\(r\)/ n\. 旅行 / tourist /ˈtʊərɪst/ n\. 游客
例句：Homestay is a great way to experience local life beyond tourism\.
译文：寄宿家庭是跳出常规旅游、体验本地生活的绝佳方式。

3. **teenager /ˈtiːneɪdʒə\(r\)/ n\.**
**English Definition**：a person aged between 13 and 19 years old
**中文释义**：青少年（13–19岁）
完整词族：teen /tiːn/ n\. 青少年时期 / teenage /ˈtiːneɪdʒ/ adj\. 青少年的
例句：Teenagers are always hungry, and they will eat you out of house and home if you let them\.
译文：青少年总是很能吃，放任不管的话会把家里吃得一干二净。

4. **abbreviation /əˌbriːviˈeɪʃn/ n\.**
**English Definition**：a shortened form of a word or phrase used in writing and speaking
**中文释义**：缩写；简写形式
完整词族：abbreviate /əˈbriːvieɪt/ v\. 缩写、简写
拓展辨析：short for 是……的缩写 / stand for 代表、象征
例句：“dorm” is the common abbreviation for “dormitory”\.
译文：dorm 是 dormitory 的常用缩写形式。

##### 2\. Core Verbs 核心动词

1. **adapt /əˈdæpt/ v\.**
**English Definition**：to adjust oneself to suit a new environment, situation, or lifestyle
**中文释义**：（为新环境）适应；调整；改造
完整词族：adaptation /ˌædæpˈteɪʃn/ n\. 适应；改编本 / adaptable /əˈdæptəbl/ adj\. 能适应的；灵活的 / adaptability /əˌdæptəˈbɪləti/ n\. 适应能力
固定搭配：adapt to new lifestyles 适应新的生活方式
听力原句：Homestays can be uncomfortable because you have to adapt to new lifestyles\.
译文：寄宿生活可能会不自在，因为你必须适应全新的生活方式。

2. **adjust /əˈdʒʌst/ v\.**
**English Definition**：to make small changes to oneself, plans or conditions to achieve a better fit or balance
**中文释义**：（主动）调整；微调；调适（心态、作息、计划、设备、生活状态）
完整词族：adjustment /əˈdʒʌstmənt/ n\. 调整，调适 / adjustable /əˈdʒʌstəbl/ adj\. 可调节的
固定搭配：adjust to new lifestyles 适应新生活 / adjust to changes 适应变化
例句：It takes time to adjust to a new homestay environment\.
译文：适应全新的寄宿家庭环境需要一段时间。
**🔥 三词考场终极辨析（adapt / adopt / adjust）**
1\. **adapt**：被动适应外界环境，为融入新环境**改变自身习惯**。
2\. **adjust**：主动、精细微调心态与状态，属于**自我优化、小幅度修正**。
3\. **adopt**：接纳外界事物，自身无改变，意为**收养；采纳**。

3. **adopt /əˈdɒpt/ v\.**
**English Definition**：to legally take another person's child into one's family; to accept and use new ideas, methods or policies
**中文释义**：收养（孩子）；采纳、采用（观点、方法、制度）
完整词族：adoption /əˈdɒpʃn/ n\. 收养；采纳 / adoptive /əˈdɒptɪv/ adj\. 收养的；有收养关系的
固定搭配：adopt a child 收养孩子 / adopt new methods 采用新方法
例句：Many families choose to adopt homeless children\.
译文：许多家庭选择收养无家可归的孩子。
**🔥 考场秒记口诀**
1\.**adapt \(a\)** = adjust 自我调整、适应（自身改变）
2\.**adopt \(o\)** = to take 外界接纳、收下（收养、采纳）

##### 3\. Core Adjectives 核心形容词

1. **responsible /rɪˈspɒnsəbl/ adj\.**
**English Definition**: being in charge of someone or something and needing to take duty for the results; reliable and trustworthy
**中文释义**：有责任的、负责的；可靠的、值得信赖的
**完整词族**：responsibility /rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪləti/ n\. 责任、职责 / irresponsible /ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsəbl/ adj\. 不负责任的
**固定搭配**：be responsible for 对……负责
**听力原句**：Host families should be responsible for planning activities for their guests\.
**译文**：寄宿家庭应当负责为入住的客人安排活动。

##### 4\. Core Adverbs 核心副词

#### Part 2 专属短语（动词短语 / 固定搭配）

1. **make it**
**DJ音标**：/meɪk ɪt/
**English Definition**：to succeed in arriving at a place on time; to succeed in doing something difficult
**中文释义**：准时抵达；成功做到（难事）
**例句**：I don’t think I can make it to the party tonight\.
**译文**：我今晚恐怕赶不上派对了。

2. **pick up**
**DJ音标**：/pɪk ʌp/
**English Definition**：to learn a skill or language informally and easily without formal study
**中文释义**：（非正式、轻松地）习得、学会（语言、技能）
**例句**：My sister picked up Russian very quickly during her time in the country\.
**译文**：我姐姐在国外期间很快就学会了俄语。

3. **laid\-back**
**DJ音标**：/ˌleɪd ˈbæk/
**English Definition**：relaxed, calm and not easily worried or upset
**中文释义**：随和的、松弛的、佛系的（形容性格、态度）
**例句**：My teacher is very laid\-back about everything\.
**译文**：我的老师对所有事情都很随和。

4. **get along with**
**DJ音标**：/ɡet əˈlɒŋ wɪð/
**English Definition**：to have a friendly and cooperative relationship with someone
**中文释义**：与……相处融洽、和睦相处
**例句**：Why don’t you get along with your roommate these days?
**译文**：你最近为什么和室友相处得不融洽？

5. **give you a ride to**
**DJ音标**：/ɡɪv juː ə raɪd tuː/
**English Definition**：to drive someone to a place in one’s own car
**中文释义**：开车送某人去某地、顺路搭载
**例句**：My host family will give me a ride to school every morning\.
**译文**：我的寄宿家庭每天早上开车送我上学。

6. **stand for vs\. short for**
**DJ音标**：/stænd fɔː\(r\) / ʃɔːt fɔː\(r\)/
**English Definition**：stand for: to represent a full word or phrase; short for: to be an abbreviated form of a longer word
**中文释义**：stand for：（缩写）代表、象征；short for：是……的缩写形式
**考场辨析**：
1\. short for → 侧重**形式缩写**（A 是 B 的简写）
2\. stand for → 侧重**含义指代**（A 代表 B 的完整含义）
**例句**：“Dorm” is short for “dormitory”, and it stands for student living buildings\.
**译文**：dorm 是 dormitory 的缩写，指代学生宿舍楼。

**📑 拓展科普：Overseas Government Foster Care System（国外政府寄养制度）**

**English Introduction**

Foster care is a formal government\-led child welfare system in Western countries, especially in the United States\. When local child protective services confirm child abuse, neglect or unsafe living environments in biological families, minors will be removed from their original homes and placed in licensed foster families or youth shelter facilities\. Qualified foster parents receive regular government financial subsidies to cover children’s living, food and daily care expenses\. The whole system is supervised by federal and state child welfare departments, aiming to protect minors’ safety and basic rights until the children return to their biological families or complete formal adoption\.

**中文详细科普**

国外（以美国为主）寄养制度是一套\*\*官方正规的儿童福利保障体系\*\*，由政府全权监管、落地执行。当儿童保护部门核查发现原生家庭存在虐待、忽视、监护缺失等危害未成年人安全的情况时，会强制将孩子带离原生家庭，安置到\*\*政府认证的合规寄养家庭\*\*或\*\*青少年收容庇护机构\*\*。
1\. 补贴机制：政府会按月给合法寄养家庭发放专项补贴，用于承担孩子的食宿、生活、看护等全部开销，寄养家庭属于公益帮扶\+官方补贴的合规岗位，并非无偿照顾。
2\. 监管部门：由联邦儿童家庭管理局、各州社会福利部门双层监管，统一制定寄养准入、考核、监护标准。
3\. 安置分类：低龄儿童优先安置寄养家庭，保障家庭式生活；无人接收、问题较多的青少年会安置在专业青少年收容所。
4\. 最终目的：临时安置过渡，核心目标是修复原生家庭监护问题、协助孩子回归原生家庭，或通过合法收养获得永久稳定的监护环境。

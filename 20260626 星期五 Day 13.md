## ✨ Daily Root \& Affix 今日词根词缀汇总

**1. \-tress**
English：female suffix, used to form feminine nouns corresponding to masculine nouns
中文：**阴性名词后缀**，附在男性职业名词后，指代对应的女性从业者
联动单词：actor /ˈæktə\(r\)/ \(男演员\) → actress /ˈæktrəs/ \(女演员\)
**Extended Examples 拓展例句**：
1. waiter ˈweɪtə\(r\)/ \(男服务员\) → waitress /ˈweɪtrəs/ \(女服务员\)
2. prince /prɪns/ \(王子\) → princess /ˌprɪnˈses/ \(公主\)
3. poet /ˈpəʊɪt/ \(诗人\) → poetess /ˌpəʊɪˈtes/ \(女诗人\)

## 💡 Note 3: Idioms（严格分Story）

### Story 1 ｜ 听力素材1 专属习语

**1. a balancing act**
**Meaning**：a difficult situation where you try to do several different things at the same time
**中文释义**：兼顾多项事务的难事，分身乏术的处境
**Sentence**：Working and studying at the same time is a real balancing act.

**2. high\-and\-mighty**
**Meaning**：self\-important and proud
**中文释义**：高傲自大的、目中无人的
**Sentence**：Sarah always acts high\-and\-mighty, and she thinks she is better than everyone else because she is a well\-known movie star.



### Story 2 ｜ 听力素材2 专属习语

**1. crack a book**
**Meaning**：to open books to study \(usually a negative meaning\)
**中文释义**：翻开书本学习（多用于否定，指几乎不学习）
**Sentence**：Brandon hardly ever cracked a book in high school, but he still was able to graduate.

**2. cut class**
**Meaning**：to skip school
**中文释义**：逃课、翘课
**Sentence**：Why do you always cut class? You won’t pass the class if you do.
